About My Topic

Discord, while popular, faces challenges with online harassment and cyberbullying. Although it has implemented tools to combat these issues, many users still struggle to find the resources they need to stay safe. My website aims to centralize information and strategies for users to protect themselves and create a respectful, inclusive community.

Cyberbullying is a serious issue, affecting millions of users worldwide.

Impact!

Long-term effects of cyberbullying include emotional trauma, social withdrawal, and mental health issues.

Where can this problem have lasting effects?

Cyberbullying can result in long-term emotional trauma, affecting users' mental health and their ability to engage online safely.

Where else can this problem have lasting effects?

In extreme cases, cyberbullying can lead to users leaving platforms, reducing diversity and fostering toxic online environments.
